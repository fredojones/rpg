Actions
=======

All actions are of syntax

    "action" : { "type" : "...", ... }

Damage
------

    "type" : "damage"

Fields:

    "subject" : "player"

Who to damage (right now only player supported)

    "amount" : ...

Amount to damage for


Say
---

    "type" : "say"

Fields:

    "message" : "..."

Message to announce
